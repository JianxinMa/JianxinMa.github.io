Biography

I am a second-year Master's student in Computer Science and Technology at Tsinghua University. I am advised by Prof. Wenwu Zhu and Prof. Peng Cui. I received my B.E. degree in Computer Science and Technology at Tsinghua University in 2017.

Research Interests

I develop machine learning techniques for analyzing and mining social networks. In particular, I have been working on network representation learning. I am interested in combining nonparametric probabilistic methods and deep learning, so as to derive high-performing techniques that are flexible and interpretable.
